Bespoke Project

Unwrapping experience for Hallmark


For most of the people same as for my user (persona), experinces are more precious than physical objects. Indeed it is a great feeling to recieve a card and feel reconnected with relatives or friends. However this feeling last just for a moment. Purpose of my design is to recall the memories that could not be packed in a form of simple postcard. With the gamification of the whole experience I was able to build the excitement, intensify the emotions and prolong the feeling of conection with the giver. The main idea of my design can be used by either friends or family and can be personalized to any extend. Even though giver is in charge of chosing the memories and tasks used in the unwrapping experience reciever is bound to reply and create his side of the experience. By exchange of reminiscences the bond is built and whole process of unwrapping is enriched and deepened.
