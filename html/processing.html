Processing

Creative Coding


As a part of my studies at THUAS I got a chance to get familiar with the Processing. Our task was to create an interactive image. In a short period of time I acknowledge the basics of programming and I was able to see how much work is behind the simplest actions and animations and how hard is to actualy think in the simple way.For me it is interesting that in programming you can truly see how complex is human brain and how hard it is for us to simplify our demands. I was inspired by the most famous Joy Division cover and made a crooked line that changes colours with the mouse movement.
