User Trialing

Rijksmuseum Studio Testing

We followed the user trialling technique, which is the observation of people using a product and collection of comments from people who have used a product. Additionally, we implemented the creative technique “thought bubble”. We made 5 different research questions, and at the end of the trial, our goal was to answer them. We presented our results in a couple different ways, by having 5 different research questions, it gave us the freedom to present our results in a plethora of different ways.
